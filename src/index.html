WE COEXIST

WHAT WE ARE ABOUT

COspace is a co-working community which allows shared office space with other creative and active individuals. A place where individuals, freelancers, start ups as well as artists, day traders, writers etc. can coexist with one another on creative ideas and much more.

CO-WORKING?

WHAT IS IT?

Coworking is a style of work that involves a shared workspace. A workspace shared by indivduals from differents fields.

COspace's roots grow from the values of community and the foundations of coexisting. The premises of networking and collaborating ideas with one another, is what we represent and pride ourselves in.

We encourage individuals from all walks of life to conversate with each other. A great insentive for this is our coffee and tea station, wine and bar area and our pingpong tables. Our spaces are open and breathable, which encourages people to explore .
